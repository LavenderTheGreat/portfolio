- WHO AM I -

I am a designer, artist and programmer from England. The majority of my inspiration comes from the media I adore and that has shaped who I am, being the joys of the consoles that I grew up with and classic culture.

Over time I grew to love the complete opposite too, being modern design with bold sharp edges and colours.

These elements form the essence of my art. Bold distinctive colours mixed with designs of old.



- THE PROCESS -

I run a four-tool process using Blender, Photoshop, Medibang and Aseprite.

With Medibang I have immediate access to familiar sketching tools I use to plan out my illustrations and designs, then I go straight to refining them.

In Aseprite, I'm granted access to a variety of tools that I use to bring out a unique pixel style.

With Photoshop and Blender I refine, frame and animate my work.

Before all of this, I go through lots of ideas I have to find the most appealing I have to offer for both myself and others. These range from vast fictional universes to faint memories of the areas that inspire me throughout my experience with other media.
